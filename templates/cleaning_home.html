Why £30 for two hours

That’s where my own cleaner says the sweet spot is. Two hours works better for travelling between jobs — and it’s essentially where the market is. Go higher and people simply don’t use you. So we price for reach and volume, not for a fat margin on paper.

Every pound to cleaners

I will put all the money straight back into cleaner fees. I am not going to draw any money from this business. Grow it indefinitely: more jobs, more customers, more surface area for the software.

Jobs + software customers

The ambition is dual: give people work, and find customers for the software company. Cleaning is the wedge into field services — real routes, real schedules, real proof.

The flywheel

Start with volume domestic and charity-linked work. Climb into higher-value commercial work. Donate surplus back. Power the cycle again.
