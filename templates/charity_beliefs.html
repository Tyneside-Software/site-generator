I have tried to construct a belief system for myself by connecting the dots between the religions I saw around the world. Most of the metaphors translate. I have chosen to cast mine in Star Wars clothes — not as fandom cosplay, but because those stories already carry the warnings I need my family to remember.

What this is

This section holds the living philosophy that guides the family — a personal faith of balance, legacy, and riding the wave across generations.

It is not a list of rules to preach. It is a map for how we try to live and pass things on. The full document is one level deeper.

The two warnings

We chase what Anakin could have been. We refuse what Luke became.

Anakin’s fall

Power and bloodline — then fear, attachment, and the hunger for control. The wave grew enormous and turned dark. What was meant to protect destroyed. The line broke.

Luke’s overcorrection

Purity and light. In many ways he succeeded where Anakin failed — then went too far the other way. Detached from necessary power and necessary darkness. No children. No continuation. The line ended with him.

Both tried to fix what came before. Both overshot. We chase the true chosen one — balance so potential can continue and improve across generations. Hot, bright, and long — without shadow corruption or sterile light.

Core tenets (short map)

  • The Wave — everything rises and falls; ride the growing edge; don’t try to stop the sea
  • Hot, bright, and long — intensity, worth, and generational reach; refuse trading one away
  • Light and Dark — order vs change; not good vs evil; complementary; hold both high
  • Maximise the product — not a lukewarm middle; when either factor hits zero, output dies
  • Ride the swing — absorb pressure without collapse so the line improves
  • Do, or do not — half-measures are falling off the wave
  • Freedom and embodiment — freely chosen, then lived; living it matters most
  • Story as transmission — pass reactions under pressure, not abstract rules

Yin and yang, middle ways, narrow paths — the tension is ancient. We say it through the wave and the product across generations.

The core creed (one breath)

We chase the true chosen one — balanced potential that can continue. We ride the sustainable wave at the growing edge. We maximise light and dark without letting either collapse. We reject both Anakin’s shadow and Luke’s sterility. We choose freely and embody it. Belief matters. Intent matters more. Living it matters most.

We burn hot, bright, and long. We tell good stories that continue. We pass the fire. Do, or do not. There is no try.
